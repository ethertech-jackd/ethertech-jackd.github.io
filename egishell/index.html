About

What EGISHELL does

EGISHELL provides hands-on technical services combining systems engineering, open-source software support (including FreeBSD), RF and embedded tinkering, and creative digital media production. We blend practical troubleshooting, product evaluation, and educational content to deliver reliable, explainable, and usable outcomes.

Core Services

  • Systems & Infrastructure: FreeBSD/Linux server setup, network architecture, forensic diagnostics, backup and recovery planning.
  • RF & Embedded Engineering: SDR-based signal prototyping, field testing, sensor integration, low-latency edge compute (Jetson/Orin, Raspberry/Beagle class nodes).
  • Product Reviews & Content: Amazon Vine & YouTube reviews, educational explainers, and technical demonstration media.
  • Consulting & Training: Practical workshops, remote troubleshooting, and custom build guidance for makers, teams, and small businesses.
