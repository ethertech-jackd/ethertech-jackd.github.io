What are you building?

  • Live/work studios for artists, hackers, and builders
  • Renewable energy systems and green building demos
  • Hoop houses and indoor food production
  • Coworking + teaching spaces for local impact
  • Performance and event venues in repurposed gym/stage areas
  • A live-in immersive arts exhibition with an AirBnB (yes)



Why here?

  • Clintonville!? Why? Rural real estate = undervalued opportunity
  • Wisconsin winters = ideal for passive solar + green heating
  • Cultural burnout = we need creative, grounding community
  • Climate urgency = time for action
  • People need places worth staying for

Who are you looking for?

  • Founding collaborators and residents
  • Green tech companies (real ones, not greenwashed)
  • Local businesses & trades
  • Donors, foundations, and grantmakers
  • Artists, teachers, farmers, engineers

What do you need?

Seeking partners, supporters, and those ready to **co-create** a new kind of rural node. If you’ve ever said “I wish there was a place like this,” you just found it.
